## WHAT WE ARE BUILDING

DeAssists is an AI-first Education ERP SaaS platform
built to run expat services and university operations
end-to-end — with minimal human staff, maximum AI
automation, and the ability to be sold to any
education or expat services business globally.

We started as a vertically integrated services
operator for students moving to Germany.
We are evolving into the operating system that runs
education services businesses — an ecosystem for
expats and universities alike.

The aim is not to grow the team.
The aim is to scale operations through AI agents
while keeping a lean, focused human team.

## THE FIVE LAYERS

### Layer 1 — Student Portal (LIVE TODAY)
Students apply, upload documents, track their
application and manage their entire journey
to Germany — all inside the portal.

Staff use the same platform to:
  — Manage incoming applications
  — Coordinate all services per student
  — Track application status across
    all 19 partner universities
  — Communicate with students and partners

Already live. Actively used by DeAssists staff,
BCBT staff, and students daily.

### Layer 2 — Lead & Sales System (BUILDING NOW)
Internal staff manage leads, run call center
operations, access sales guides, track conversions
and manage commissions.
Completely replaces Google Sheets.

INCLUDES:
  Universal Lead Capture Form
  — Single embeddable contact form
  — Works on website, landing pages,
    partner sites, social media,
    WhatsApp links, any platform
  — Every submission creates a live
    CRM lead automatically
  — Auto-routes to correct queue
    by source and service selected
  — Zero manual entry required

### Layer 3 — Finance Module (NEXT)
Fee tracking, invoices, commissions,
payment reconciliation.
Foundation for DATEV connection.
Connects to Layer 2 commission tracking.
Test using BCBT data first — understand
their fee structure fully before building.
Architecture first, prototype second,
UI/UX review third, then build.

### Layer 4 — SMS + LMS (FUTURE — BCBT FIRST)
Student Management System for universities:
  — Attendance, classes, timetables
  — Exam management, results, certificates

Learning Management System:
  — Course content delivery
  — Assessments, progress tracking
  — Certificates

BUILD APPROACH:
  Step 1 — Go into BCBT. Understand their
           current operations fully.
  Step 2 — Create clear architecture.
  Step 3 — Build prototype.
  Step 4 — Review UI/UX against existing portal.
  Step 5 — Finalise. Then build.

Target: SMS + LMS MVP live for BCBT
September 2026 intake.

This layer turns DeAssists into a full
university operating system.

The goal: operate a university with minimal
staff and excellent educators — AI handles
all routine operations. A future-oriented
educational system.

### Layer 5 — Tax & Compliance (FUTURE)
DATEV integration for German tax compliance.
Financial reporting for university finance teams.
Internal fee-to-tax pipeline.
Makes DeAssists compliant for any German
education or expat services business.

BUILD APPROACH:
  Same as Layer 4 — understand BCBT's
  tax and compliance needs first.
  Architecture then prototype then UI review
  then build.

## THE DUAL-MODE PRINCIPLE

Every feature works in two modes simultaneously:

OPERATOR MODE — DeAssists internal view
  Full pipeline across all tenants
  All students, all services, all partners
  Full permissions, internal tools

PLATFORM MODE — Tenant view
  University, partner, or service provider
  Scoped to their own pipeline only
  Cannot see other tenants
  Same UI, configured data
  Same permissions model, scoped data

Tenant = any of:
  — A university (BCBT, others)
  — An expat service provider
    (accommodation, visa, insurance etc)
  — A partner agency

Service context switcher at the top.
Same shell. Scoped data.

## BUILD PRINCIPLES (non-negotiable)

1. Tenant-aware from day one
   Every feature configurable per organisation
   No hardcoded DeAssists-specific logic
   What works for DeAssists must work
   for BCBT and the next 50 universities

2. AI-hookable at every action
   Every state change fires an event
   AI agents can act without human involvement
   Design for automation from day one

3. Constants before components
   Enums exist before code references them
   Constants file is a hard gate
   No magic strings anywhere

4. No hardcoded business values
   Statuses, queues, roles, services,
   fees, rules — all configurable per tenant

5. Four-layer API chain — no exceptions
   Component then Hook then Core Hook
   then Axios then Backend

6. Operator today, platform tomorrow
   Every decision supports both simultaneously

7. AI-first design
   If a human does it repetitively today,
   design it so AI can do it tomorrow
   without a rebuild

8. Architecture before prototype
   For every new layer:
   Understand then Architecture then Prototype
   then UI/UX review then Finalise then Build
