## THE 9-YEAR MAP

### 2026 — Foundation
  DeAssists ERP live for internal team.
  BCBT as first external tenant.
  10 universities on platform.
  AI handles lead routing automatically.
  Call center agents focus on relationships.
  The portal trains itself.

### 2027 — Expansion
  50 universities globally.
  AI voice agent handles initial student contact.
  Human agents handle only complex cases.
  Finance module fully automated.
  Student mobile app is primary touchpoint.
  Universities self-onboard in under 1 hour.

### 2028 — Platform
  100 universities globally.
  DeAssists is infrastructure — not a service.
  White-label available for any education brand.
  Human touch reserved for exceptions only.
  Revenue model shifts to platform fees.

### 2030 — Operating System
  DeAssists is the operating system
  for international education services.
  Any university. Any country.
  Any student journey. Fully AI-driven.
  Compliance, visa, accommodation, finance —
  all handled by connected AI agents.

### 2034 — Infrastructure
  The Stripe of education services.
  API-first. AI-native. Global.
  Students interact with AI exclusively
  unless they choose human contact.
  Universities are tenants on a global platform.
  DeAssists is invisible infrastructure
  that makes international education work.

## WHAT THIS MEANS FOR HOW WE BUILD TODAY

Every feature must be:

AI-HOOKABLE
  Every state change fires an event.
  Every event an AI agent can act on.
  We build the human workflow first.
  AI replaces it step by step.

DATA-RICH
  Every interaction creates a data point.
  Every data point feeds future intelligence.
  We never throw away data.
  Every field we add today is
  a training signal for tomorrow.

TENANT-AWARE
  Every feature works for any university.
  No DeAssists-specific assumptions.
  Configuration over code. Always.
  A new university is a database entry.
  Not a deployment.

SELF-EXPLAINING
  The portal trains users automatically.
  No human training required.
  A new staff member on day one
  can use every feature without help.

COMPOSABLE
  Every module works standalone.
  Every module connects to others.
  A university can use just CRM.
  Or just Student Portal.
  Or just Finance.
  Or all of them.
  Each works. All work better together.
