1. SUPER_ADMIN — sees every internal item (8 top-level, all children).
2. ORG_OWNER — sees Call Center 369 with 5 children, Sales CRM with 1 child, Service Setup, Applications, Manage User Accounts. **Does not** see Finance, Mission Control.

4. TEAM_LEAD — sees Dashboard, Call Center 369 (5 children including Service Catalog + Sales Library), Applications, Manage User Accounts (3 of 5 leaves). **Does not** see Sales CRM, Service Setup.
5. STAFF — sees Dashboard + Applications only.
6. AGENT, ORG_AGENT — sees Dashboard, Services (agent menu), Applications, Manage User Accounts > Service Users only. **Never** sees Call Center 369, Sales CRM, Service Setup, Finance, Mission Control.
7. Three-layer audit (Sidebar + Page guard + Data permission) PASS for every role.
