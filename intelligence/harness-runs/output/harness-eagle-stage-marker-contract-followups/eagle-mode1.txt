The harness is **brain-repo internal tooling, written 8 hours ago, that we explicitly need to evolve v1 → v2.**

**Rule cited:** EAGLESKILL.md "ADD-ONLY RULE" + "WHEN ASKED TO MODIFY EXISTING LOGIC" — EAGLE refuses.

**Options:**

- **A. Lock a scoped exemption** in `memory/decisions.md`:
  > *"EAGLE add-only rule does not apply to harness/* (brain-repo internal tooling) when the harness itself is the subject of the ticket. Production portal code remains add-only."*

  Then proceed with all 8 items as in-place rewrites where the ticket calls for it. Cleanest, smallest diff, no v1/v2 cruft.

- **B. Pure add-only rewrite** — keep v1 paths intact, add v2 paths alongside (`MODE3_STAGES_V2`, `runSlashCommandV2`, `validatePatchV2`, etc.), then flip a flag at top of `eagle-harness.js`. More files, more cruft, no real safety gain because the harness has zero external dependents.

**What I recommend:** **Option A**. The add-only rule's purpose is to protect users; the harness has zero users besides Shon. A scoped, documented exemption is honest about what the rule is for. The exemption is narrow (`harness/**` only, never portal, never brain memory/decisions/patterns).
